Nate van der Ende (*1987, Canada) is a London-based developer and digital designer. He operates internationally within a broad range of sectors and frequently collaborates with designers and artists to produce memorable and engaging work.

He is Chief Technical Officer and Creative Lead at ARTBLX, co-curator of Hover States and runs a joint digital practice with Johanna Lundberg: andjohanna.co.

For more information and proposals, please contact nate@van-der-en.de
